### Part B

As is vividly depicted in the captivating cartoon, an exhilarating dragon boat race is in full swing on a roaring river, with two decorated boats competing fiercely. On the riverbank stands a crowd of cheered villagers, among whom two elderly spectators are staring at the race with broad smiles. One of them cannot help exclaiming, "How wonderful! The dragon boat race in our village is becoming increasingly lively!" Simple as the illustration is, the symbolic meaning it conveys is both deep and thought-provoking.

The core message implied behind the drawing is the roaring revival of traditional Chinese culture driven by rural revitalization. On the one hand, with the booming economy and improved living standards in rural areas, farmers are no longer merely satisfied with material wealth; instead, they are passionately pursuing a richer spiritual and cultural life. On the other hand, traditional festivals serve as an spiritual bond that enhances cultural confidence and tightens community integration. The lively scene of the dragon boat race eloquently proves that our time-honored heritage is gaining renewed vitality in modern society.

From my personal perspective, this heartening trend should be highly valued and further promoted. Not only should local governments invest more resources to preserve folk customs, but young generations must also shoulder the responsibility of passing them down. Only in this way can we foster a culturally prosperous society.
